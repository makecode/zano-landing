Zano Manual

Zano mining is done by specialized computers. The role of miners is to secure the network and to process every Zano transaction. Miners achieve this by solving a computational problem which allows them to chain together blocks of transactions (hence Zano’s famous “blockchain”). For this service, miners are rewarded with newly-created Zano and transaction fees.

Miners secure the Zano network by making it difficult to attack, alter or stop. The more miners that mine, the more the secure the network.
